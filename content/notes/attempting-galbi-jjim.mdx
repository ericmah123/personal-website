Galbi jjim has been on my list for a while. Finally committed to a Sunday cook.
The prep alone took an hour — soaking the ribs, blanching, prepping the vegetables.
Four hours of braising later and the result was genuinely good. The ribs were falling
off the bone. The sauce was rich and slightly sweet. Will make again.
